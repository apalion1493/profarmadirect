Description:

Differin is a brand name for a medication containing the active ingredient adapalene. It is primarily used to treat acne.

Use for:

Adapalene, the active ingredient in Differin, works by binding to specific receptors in the skin cells called retinoic acid receptors. It regulates the growth and differentiation of skin cells, preventing the formation of clogged pores, and reducing inflammation. This helps to improve acne by promoting the turnover of skin cells and preventing the formation of acne lesions.
